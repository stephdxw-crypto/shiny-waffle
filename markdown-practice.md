# How to Sell on Wallapop

Wallapop is a **mobile marketplace** where you can sell things you no longer need. It's popular in Spain and works great for getting rid of furniture, clothes, electronics — *basically anything*.

> "One person's junk is another person's treasure." That's the whole idea behind Wallapop.

---

## Getting Started

First, you need to download the app and create an account. Here's what you'll need:
 
- A smartphone (iOS or Android)
- An email address or Google account
- A profile photo (builds trust with buyers)

Visit the official site to learn more: [Wallapop](https://www.wallapop.com)

---

## Steps to List Your First Item

Follow these steps to publish your first listing:
 
1. Open the app and tap the **green "Sell" button**
2. Take clear photos of your item (at least 3)
3. Write a *descriptive* title and description
4. Set a fair price — check similar listings first
5. Choose a category and tap **Publish**

### Writing a Good Description
 
A good description answers three questions: what is it, what condition is it in, and why are you selling it. Here's an example:

```
Title: IKEA KALLAX Shelf — White, great condition
Description: Selling my KALLAX shelf (4x2). Used for one year,
no scratches. Selling because I'm moving. Pick up in Eixample.
Price: €40 (negotiable)
```

You can also use `#hashtags` in your description to help buyers find your item.

---

## Pricing Tips
 
| Condition       | Suggested Discount |
| --------------- | ------------------ |
| Like new        | 20–30% off retail  |
| Good            | 40–50% off retail  |
| Used but works  | 60–70% off retail  |
| For parts only  | 80%+ off retail    |

## Final Advice
 
Selling on Wallapop is easy once you get the hang of it. The key things to remember are:
 
1. **Good photos** sell items faster
2. *Be honest* about the condition
3. Respond to messages quickly
 
Happy selling! 🎉
